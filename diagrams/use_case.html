Key Use Cases Included:

  • Authentication & Verification: Signing up, logging in, and the mandatory ID verification process.
  • Post Management: Creating, viewing, editing, deleting posts, and searching for content.
  • Interaction & Reputation: Voting, replying, and managing profiles, including the impact on reputation.
  • Filtering & Sorting: Location-based filtering, popularity and recency sorting of content.
